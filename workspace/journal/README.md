# The Living Journal — SCRIBE Workspace

## Founder + AI Working Together

This directory is the journal.  It is not a folder of files — it is the body
of the SCRIBE organism.  Every document placed here is ingested, classified,
and synthesized.

### How It Works

1. **Feed.**  Place a document in this directory.  Speak into it.  The
   founder talks — Scribe listens.  Every sentence is architecture.

2. **Classify.**  Scribe reads the document and assigns it to one of eight
   golden-section categories:

   | Partition | Category       | Weight | Purpose                        |
   |-----------|----------------|--------|--------------------------------|
   | Major (φ) | Research        | 1.000  | Formal research and findings   |
   | Major (φ) | Architecture    | 0.618  | Structural design and patterns |
   | Major (φ) | Theory          | 0.382  | Theoretical frameworks         |
   | Major (φ) | Vision          | 0.236  | Forward-looking direction      |
   | Minor (1/φ)| Implementation | 0.146  | Technical execution            |
   | Minor (1/φ)| Synthesis      | 0.090  | Cross-document intelligence    |
   | Minor (1/φ)| Chronicle      | 0.056  | Historical record              |
   | Minor (1/φ)| Genesis        | 0.034  | Founding documents             |

   Weights follow the golden ratio decay: each is φ⁻¹ of the previous.

3. **Generate.**  Scribe synthesizes research-paper-worthy papers from
   accumulated documents within each category.  Papers include abstract,
   structured sections, and source citations.

4. **Grow.**  The journal advances generations at Fibonacci thresholds.
   Generation 0 → 1 at the 1st document.  Gen 1 → 2 at the 2nd.  Then 3rd,
   5th, 8th, 13th, 21st, 34th, 55th, 89th…  Each generation shift is a
   growth event for the organism.
